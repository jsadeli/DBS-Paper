    About

    A modern, APA-based, Doshisha-centric, and professional-looking paper template for all your writings at DBS. Designed to support color or black-and-white printing, while maximizing readability on computer screens.

    Formats

    Template files are currently available for Microsoft Office Word, Apple iWork Pages, and Google Docs.
